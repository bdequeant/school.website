Student Clubs

Six of Hancock High School’s Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) members traveled to Jackson, Miss., last week for Mississippi’s state FBLA conference. Each of the student participated in various competitive events including Word Processing (Alexis Douglas), Future Business Leader (Sydney Lee), Global Business (Max Anderson), Introduction to Parliamentary Procedure (Hannah Davis), Marketing (Bradley Wilson) and Organizational Leadership (Tommy Gilbert). Two students, Alexis Douglas and Sydney Lee, won second place in their respective events, earning them an invitation to compete at FBLA’s National Leadership Conference this summer in Baltimore, Md. “Our students performed and represented their school very well, as they always do,” said Mrs. Parnell, Hancock’s FBLA adviser. “I couldn’t be more proud of them.”
